#+TITLE: Legendre transform
* Summary :SUMMARY:
* Questions :QUESTIONS:
* Notes
  :LOGBOOK:
  CLOCK: [2021-07-23 Fri 13:00]
  :END:

  This is a way of converting quantities which depend on one quantity,
  to a conjugate property.

  Examples of this are

  | From        | To       |
  |-------------+----------|
  | Position    | Momentum |
  | Pressure    | Volume   |
  | Temperature | Entropy  |
